Case Study

Global KYC Transformation for a Multi-National Bank

Led an enterprise-wide KYC transformation for a multi-national bank, building a new target operating model and helping translate KYC execution across EMEA, APAC, and America.

Challenge

The bank's KYC operations had evolved region-by-region, creating fragmented workflows, duplicated controls, and high operating cost. Leadership needed a single global model that could still meet local regulatory requirements across major markets.

Approach

  • Designed a unified KYC target operating model covering onboarding, periodic refresh, trigger events, and escalation pathways.
  • Defined global policy standards with jurisdiction-specific overlays for EMEA, APAC, and America to preserve local compliance alignment.
  • Built transformation governance with business, compliance, operations, and technology owners aligned to common KPIs and delivery milestones.

Execution

  • Served as global program lead, directing implementation across regional teams and helping translate KYC execution across EMEA, APAC, and America based on risk and business criticality.
  • Partnered with technology teams to implement the new KYC model into shared platforms, workflows, and data controls across lines of business.
  • Led cross-regional change management and operating model adoption to ensure process consistency and sustainable execution.

Outcomes

  • Delivered a global KYC operating model deployed across EMEA, APAC, and America sectors.
  • Enabled an estimated annual savings of nearly $500M through process standardization, workflow redesign, and technology implementation.
  • Improved control consistency, auditability, and execution speed across the enterprise KYC lifecycle.
  • Established a scalable foundation for future risk and compliance modernization programs.

Capabilities Demonstrated

  • Global KYC operating model transformation
  • Cross-region regulatory strategy execution
  • Enterprise technology implementation leadership
  • Large-scale operating model deployment
  • KPI-driven cost and control optimization
