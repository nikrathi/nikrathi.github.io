Case Study

AI-Driven AML Transformation

Reframing AML operations as a product platform to improve detection quality, regulatory confidence, and execution speed.

Challenge

A high-growth fintech needed to modernize AML monitoring while navigating exam pressure and remediation expectations. Existing workflows were fragmented, generated heavy manual effort, and reduced confidence in risk signal quality.

Approach

  • Built a product roadmap aligning compliance, data, engineering, and operations around prioritized control outcomes.
  • Introduced AI/analytics-supported monitoring layers integrated with a broad AML rule stack and clearer governance checkpoints.
  • Established program-level KPIs to track review throughput, signal quality, and remediation progress.

Execution

  • Led cross-functional delivery in phased releases to reduce change risk and maintain control continuity.
  • Coordinated model validation and risk taxonomy alignment to support regulatory auditability.
  • Embedded reporting and controls into operations to improve consistency for leadership and exam stakeholders.

Outcomes

  • Contributed to successful exam and remediation progress.
  • Reduced false positives and analyst manual effort by about 25%.
  • Improved decision support by integrating analytics with 80+ AML rules.
  • Strengthened trust in program execution through measurable control performance indicators.

Capabilities Demonstrated

  • Regulatory transformation leadership
  • AML product strategy and lifecycle management
  • Model governance and risk taxonomy alignment
  • Cross-functional operating model design
  • KPI-driven compliance performance management
