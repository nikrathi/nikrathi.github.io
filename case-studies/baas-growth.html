Case Study

BaaS Growth with Embedded Controls

Designing compliance patterns that scale-enabling fast embedded finance launches while protecting AML and fraud control quality across multiple payment rails.

Challenge

BaaS platforms needed to onboard clients quickly across ACH, card, and real-time payments-without creating inconsistent controls, manual workarounds, or gaps in monitoring and escalation.

Approach

  • Designed reusable control patterns for onboarding and ongoing monitoring across payment rails (ACH, card, real-time).
  • Mapped regulatory requirements into product workflows so implementation didn't rely on manual interpretation by each team.
  • Sequenced delivery using risk tiers to prioritize the highest-impact controls and accelerate safe launches.

Execution

  • Led product, compliance, and engineering delivery through Agile/Scrum increments-balancing growth timelines with regulatory obligations.
  • Implemented governance and monitoring consistency across three major BaaS products-clarifying ownership, escalation paths, and reporting.
  • Built performance reporting to track onboarding quality, monitoring effectiveness, and control health over time.

Outcomes

  • Enabled onboarding of 10+ strategic clients ahead of schedule.
  • Supported ~20% revenue acceleration by reducing launch friction while maintaining compliance strength.
  • Strengthened financial crime prevention posture through standardized controls and measurable accountability.
  • Reduced rework and operational risk by embedding controls into the product experience rather than relying on manual processes.

Capabilities Demonstrated

  • BaaS controls strategy and architecture
  • Payment-rail aware compliance design (ACH, card, RTP)
  • Risk-tiered product roadmap prioritization
  • Cross-functional Agile/Scrum delivery leadership
  • KPI-driven governance and performance reporting
