Case Study

Trade Surveillance Product Platform

Replacing manual surveillance reporting with automated, KPI-led supervision-improving audit readiness, response time, and operational reliability.

Challenge

Trade surveillance oversight relied heavily on manual reporting and fragmented review routines. This created delays in issue identification, inconsistent escalation, and limited supervisory visibility-especially under audit and regulatory scrutiny.

Approach

  • Defined a product vision for supervision-standard KPIs, automated reporting, and consistent escalation/audit trails.
  • Translated manual supervisory routines into a reusable product model (thresholds, prioritization logic, drill-down views, evidence capture).
  • Established success metrics tied to audit readiness, timeliness, and operational burden reduction.

Execution

  • Led cross-functional delivery across surveillance, compliance, and technology-shipping incrementally using Agile practices.
  • Modernized and optimized 150+ regulatory reports to improve consistency, responsiveness, and control confidence.
  • Implemented KPI and performance indicator routines so the platform could be managed as a living product, not a one-time release.

Outcomes

  • Improved audit readiness through automated reporting and standardized surveillance KPIs.
  • Reduced manual compliance overhead by replacing spreadsheet-driven processes with repeatable workflows.
  • Improved responsiveness and timeliness of supervisory reporting and escalation.
  • Created a scalable foundation for future surveillance enhancements and regulator-ready reporting.

Capabilities Demonstrated

  • Trade surveillance product strategy and KPI architecture
  • Manual-to-automated reporting transformation
  • Supervisory dashboard design and governance
  • Cross-functional Agile delivery
  • Audit-ready workflow and evidence design
